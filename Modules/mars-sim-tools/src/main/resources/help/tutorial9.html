Tutorial - Step 9 : Settlement Map

The Settlement Map allows you to zoom in on a particular settlement and see the whereabout of the settlers, bots inside/outside the buildings or vehicles.

You can select which settlement you want to view from the drop-down list on the top right.

The map can be zoomed in and out with the zoom slider, and rotated right or left with the rotate buttons.

YOu may drag around the map by the right mouse button.

The Recenter button will reset the map at the settlement's center, default zoom, and rotation.

The checkboxes allows the user to select which types of labels be overlay.

The types of labels include daylight tracking, buildings, construction sites, vehicles, people and bots.

Left clicking on a person or a bot will display their current activities.

Right click on a person, bot, building or vehicle will give you the menu options of Info, Details or Move elsewhere.
