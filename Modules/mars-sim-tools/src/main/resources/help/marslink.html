MarsLink

MarsLink is a chat box located at the left end of the Status Bar.

It is a new feature that allows users to interact with the 'A.I. agent' via text that inhabits the MarsLink, a smart globalnet on future Mars.

In the prompt, enter the name of a person, a bot, a settlement, or a vehicle to initiate your query.

Once the connection has been established, enter specific commands to interact the the A.I. agent.

For details, please do take a look at our MarsLink wiki online.

Note : you may type '0' or 'quit' or 'bye' or 'exit' and hit enter to leave the conversation.
