Commander Tool

The Commander tool displays a dashboard of information pertaining to policies and decisions made at the leadership leve.

There are eight tabs, each representing division or department.

  • Agriculture - pertaining to growing crop and food production of a settlement.
  • Computing - pertaining to the use of computing resources of a settlement.
  • Diplomatic - pertaining to how a settlement may moderate its relationship with other Mars settlements or Moon colonies.
  • Engineering - pertaining to the development projects of a settlement.
  • Logistic - pertaining to the assignment of specific task orders for settlers.
  • Mission - pertaining to tuning how missions may impact other settlements.
  • Resource - pertaining to tuning how resources are harvested and utilized.
  • Safety - pertaining to tuning the parameters on safety and health of the settlers.
  • Science - pertaining to tuning the policy of how science are conducted.
