Settlement Map Tool

The Settlement Map Tool displays a top-down view of settlements with the layout of their buildings, parked vehicles, and people.

You can see the settlers and bots within buildings and vehicles or outside of them in the proximity of the settlement.

Left click a settler (or a bot) will select that person (or that robot).

Right click on a building, a construction site or a vehicle will bring up a pop-up menu.

For a building or a construction site, the options on that pop-up menu are Description and Details.

For a vehicle, the options on that pop-up menu are Description, Details and Relocate.

Note : when positioning a new building or construction site, if a vehicle is occupying a certain location, it is useful to choose 'Relocate' to remove the vehicle from current location to a new location.

You may click on the Settlement name box to choose and switch to another settlement.

You may zoom in/out and rotate the map by pressing the greenish '+' and '-' and radar button.

You may also use the zoom handle bar or your mouse wheel for zooming in/out.

You may use the right mouse button to drag around the map of the settlement area.

You may use the 'Layers' button to check and un-check various layers to turn it on or off

They are 6 available layers : Daylight Tracking, Buildings, Construction Sites, Vehicle, People, and Bots.

With the exception of the first layer, namely, the 'Daylight Tracking' layer, the rest of layers are simply the name label of a type of Units.
