Simulation Concepts

Mars Simulation Project (mars-sim) is a Java-based open source project that simulates the activities of the first generation of settlers on Mars.

A. Modeling and Simulation

As a general purpose computer simulation (See Modeling for details), mars-sim models a settler's career background into various abstractions such as job, role, attributes/skills, task preferences, personality/temperament and level of relationship with one another as they go about their daily lives on Mars.

Each agent is endowed with various human-like characteristics such as age, height, weight, blood types, health indices, etc., capable of making distinct decisions and interacting with one another and perform tasks and mission within the settlement and on the surface of Mars.

On a powerful machine, player may create numerous settlements spreading across Mars, from a simple 4-person initial landing base, or a mining site to more matured bases with buildings hosting various functions.

Each settler is associated with one particular settlement. Each settlement houses buildings and vehicles and has a leadership structure (i.e. commander, sub-commander, chiefs and specialists) and has a development objective which will drives its development and production into a specialization of certain economic outputs.

Settlements trade goods with one another. In each Trading Mission, goods are exchanged and Value Points (VP) are calculated for the two involving settlements as the VP system is the proto-currency or economic system used in mars-sim. Each resource worths a certain amount of VP and the can change dynamically over time based upon the demand and supply of that resource.

B. Architecture

mars-sim is composed of the following constructs or archetypes :

1. Five types of Unit

  • Structure
  • Vehicle
  • Person
  • Equipment
  • Robot

2. Three types of Structure

  • Settlements
  • Buildings
  • Construction Sites
Note : Landmarks are currently unexplorable

3. Eight types of Equipment

  • Bag
  • Barrel
  • Building Kit
  • EVA Suit
  • Gas Canister
  • Large Bag
  • Robots
  • Specimen Container

4. Five categories of Supplies

  • Buildings
  • Vehicles
  • Equipment
  • Resources
  • Parts

5. Two types of Resources

  • Item Resource
  • Amount Resource

6. One type of Item Resource

  • Parts

7. Various types of Amount Resource

  • Elements (Gas, Liquid, Solid)
  • Cooking Condiment
  • Chemical Compounds
  • Mineral Concentration
  • Building/Construction Material
  • Drugs/Supplements
  • Bio-organism
  • Crops
  • Utility items from crop harvest
  • Derived food resources from crop harvest
  • Waste (grey/black water, solid/food/toxic/electronic waste, compost)
Note : A meal is not a single Amount Resource but a composite of various Amount Resources.

8. Three types of Processes

  • Manufacturing Processes for fabricating items and complicated parts, assembling equipment/vehicles.
  • Food Productions for producing derived food products as well as non-edible items/resources for daily use.
  • Resource Processes, for generating gases and liquids and solids.

9. Two types of Ground Vehicles

  • Light Utility Vehicle (LUV)
  • Rover

10. Three types of Rovers

  • Cargo Rover
  • Explorer Rover
  • Transport Rover

11. Two types of Activities

  • Missions : 12 meta types
  • Tasks : 56 meta types (or 77 if including subtasks)

12. Medical Ailments

Persons can suffer from a various Complaints. Some of these can degrade into more serious Complaints if not cured. Cured involves applying a Treatment, some can be self applied by the Person whilst some need the use of a Medical facility.


13. Six types of Settlement Objectives

  • Crop Farm
  • Manufacturing
  • Research Center
  • Transportation Hub
  • Trade Town
  • Tourism

14. A Settler's Profile

  • Job : 16 types
  • Role : 18 types
  • Personality : (a) Five Factor Model(FFM) and (b) Myers-Briggs Type Indicator (MBTI) models
  • Natural Attributes : 14 types
  • Preference : an affinity score for each of the 56 meta tasks
  • Favorite Activities : 8 types
  • Choice of Meals (Primary & Secondary): 28 types
  • Skills : 17 types
